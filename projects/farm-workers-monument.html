Farm Workers Monument

A commemorative design and fabrication project honoring the legacy and contributions of farm workers.

Project Overview

This project focused on designing and fabricating a commemorative monument honoring national farm workers. The structure was created as both a tribute and a place of gathering, combining symbolic design with hands-on fabrication.

Design Intent

The monument was designed to represent respect, labor, memory, and community. The goal was to create a meaningful structure that could serve as a visual tribute while also functioning as a public gathering space.

Fabrication Process

The project involved multiple fabrication methods, including shielded metal arc welding, plasma cutting, and woodworking. Steel components were cut and assembled to form the monument structure, while wood elements were used for the bench and seating areas.
