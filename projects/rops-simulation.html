ROPS Structural Analysis

Finite Element Analysis of a Rollover Protective Structure designed to evaluate structural performance under multiple loading conditions.

Project Overview

This project focused on designing and evaluating a rollover protective structure (ROPS) for agricultural equipment. The objective was to determine whether the structure could safely withstand loading conditions that may occur during a rollover event while maintaining acceptable stress levels throughout the frame.

Objectives

The goal was to develop a structurally sound design and verify its performance through simulation. The analysis included vertical, longitudinal, and lateral loading scenarios commonly used when evaluating rollover protective structures.

Software & Tools

SolidWorks
SolidWorks Simulation
Finite Element Analysis
CAD Design
Structural Analysis

Engineering Analysis

Multiple simulation studies were performed to evaluate stress distribution, deformation, and overall structural behavior. Special attention was given to high-stress regions and load transfer through critical structural members.

Results indicated that the design remained below material yield limits and demonstrated acceptable structural performance under the evaluated loading cases.

Skills Demonstrated

This project strengthened my experience in finite element analysis, engineering design, structural evaluation, CAD modeling, and technical problem solving.
