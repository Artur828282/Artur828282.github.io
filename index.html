About Me

I am a Civil Engineering student at California State University, Fresno, with experience in structural analysis, CAD design, manufacturing, and fabrication. I enjoy work that combines technical thinking with hands-on building, whether that means running a simulation, creating construction drawings, welding a steel frame, or developing a physical model.


My background includes engineering internship experience, research, technical drafting, finite element analysis, welding, woodworking, 3D printing, and laser-cut model fabrication. I am especially interested in structural engineering and practical design solutions that can move from concept to real-world use.
